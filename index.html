Mary Saotome

Mary is a strategic player who rarely lets herself be carried away by emotion. Her ability to analyze the psychology of other players makes her a master at controlling resources, manipulating, and destabilizing her opponents.

Ryo Kagami

Ryo is a master at controlling the minds of others and uses this to his advantage during games. His style is silent and ruthless, never showing emotion or haste. He prefers to operate in the shadows, manipulating events so subtly that his opponents don't even realize they're already playing on his turf.

Ayame Kurozaki

Ayame is an unpredictable and daring player who specializes in manipulating the risks of any game. She excels in moments of pressure, where luck and unpredictability come into play. Ayame never follows a fixed pattern of play, preferring to constantly adapt to the flow of the game and make decisions that confuse her opponents.
